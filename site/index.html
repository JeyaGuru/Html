About

Sridhar Vembu (born 1968) is an Indian billionaire business magnate and the founder and CEO of Zoho Corporation.[2] According to Forbes, he is the 59th richest Indian in the world with a net worth of US$2.4 billion, as of May 2021.[3] He was awarded India's fourth highest civilian award, the Padma Shri, in 2021
